AMBASSADOR PROGRAM

We have made this vision a corner stone for our every step, & our mission is Empowerment through technology as in line with the vision of the Quaid and iEducate Ambassador Program is one such initiative. From our experience as the leading vocational I.T. education provider in Pakistan, we identified a void in software skill of our office going individuals, especially in MS Office which is the most widely deployed software in the Corporate Community of Pakistan, but is mostly underutilized due to lack of training/expertise. This has a dire effect on both the productivity of the individual, the organization and the nation as a whole. iEducate Ambassador Program is geared towards fulfilling this void by helping them achieve greater office productivity through 6-8 hrs. free MS Office suite training by our students.
It also gives our Students, studying in various semesters, an invaluable opportunity to get personally involved in their own community. Get a sense of both achievement & fulfillment. This program had a very humble beginning, from sending a few students to help out a school, to a massive undertaking geared towards helping office going individuals from our own community. We approach the otherwise self-neglected, who don’t have the means/time/capacity to attend/afford classes in IEDUCATE or otherwise.
